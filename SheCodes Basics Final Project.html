Friends Reunion is Officially Happening!

Jennifer Aniston, Courteney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, Matthew Perry, David Schwimmer, and Matt LeBlanc are all returning to film the special on the show’s original set at Warner Bros. Studio’s Stage 24 in Burbank, California.

Now, 17 years after the last episode aired, the cast of Friends is returning for a reunion special to reminisce about their time on the hit '90s sitcom.

HBO Max has yet to announce an official premiere date for the Friends reunion special.
